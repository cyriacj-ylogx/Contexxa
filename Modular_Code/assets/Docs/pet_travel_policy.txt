PET TRAVEL POLICY

IndiGo allows small domesticated dogs and cats to travel in the cabin on domestic flights only.

Booking Requirements
- Pet travel must be requested at least 48 hours before departure through the call center.
- A maximum of two pets are allowed per flight, on a first-come first-served basis.
- The pet must travel in a soft carrier that fits under the seat in front of you.

Weight and Carrier Rules
- The combined weight of the pet and carrier must not exceed 5 kg.
- The carrier dimensions must not exceed 45 cm x 35 cm x 25 cm.

Fees
- A pet-in-cabin fee of INR 5,000 applies per pet per sector.
- Service animals accompanying passengers with disabilities travel free of charge.
