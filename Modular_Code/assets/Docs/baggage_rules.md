# IndiGo Baggage Rules

## Carry-On Allowance

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item free of charge.

- Carry-on maximum dimensions: 22 x 14 x 9 inches
- Personal item (purse, laptop bag) must fit under the seat in front

## Checked Baggage Allowance by Fare Type

- **Lite fare**: No free checked baggage included. Checked baggage must be purchased separately.
- **Classic fare**: One free checked bag up to 15 kg included.
- **Flexi fare**: One free checked bag up to 20 kg included.

## Checked Baggage Fees

- Overweight fee (23–32 kg): INR 2,500 per bag
- Oversized fee (dimensions over 62 inches): INR 3,000 per bag

## Sports Equipment

Sports equipment such as bicycles, skis, and surfboards must be declared at check-in and attract a flat fee of INR 4,000 per item.
