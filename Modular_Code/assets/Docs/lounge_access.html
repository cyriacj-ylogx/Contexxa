Airport Lounge Access Policy

Eligibility

IndiGo passengers flying Business Class or holding a 6E Rewards Platinum membership are eligible for complimentary lounge access at all major Indian airports.

Guest Policy

Each eligible passenger may bring one guest into the lounge. Additional guests are charged INR 1,500 per person.

Facilities

The lounge offers the following facilities and amenities to all eligible passengers at no extra charge:

  • Complimentary food and beverages
  • High-speed Wi-Fi internet access
  • Shower facilities (available at selected lounges)
  • Business centre with printing and copying services

Hours

Lounges are open 2 hours before the first flight of the day until the last departure. Access is permitted up to 3 hours before your scheduled flight.
