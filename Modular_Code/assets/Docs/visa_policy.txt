VISA REQUIREMENTS

Passengers must carry a valid visa for their destination country before boarding any IndiGo international flight.

Key Rules
- A valid passport with at least 6 months validity is mandatory for all international travel.
- IndiGo staff will verify travel documents at check-in.
- Passengers without valid documentation will be denied boarding.
- Transit visas may be required depending on the layover country.

Responsibility
- It is the sole responsibility of the passenger to obtain correct travel documents.
- IndiGo will not be liable for any costs arising from incorrect or missing documents.

Transit Without Visa (TWOV)
Passengers transiting through India without a visa may be eligible for Transit Without Visa (TWOV) if they meet all of the following conditions:
- Holding a confirmed onward ticket departing within 24 hours.
- Remaining in the international transit area at all times.
- Holding a valid visa for the final destination country.
- Nationals of countries eligible under India's TWOV scheme.
Passengers who do not meet these conditions must obtain an Indian transit visa before travel.

Tourist Visa Duration by Country
- India: 90 days on e-Tourist Visa (eTV); multiple entry permitted.
- UAE: 30-day or 60-day tourist visa available on arrival or in advance.
- United Kingdom: Up to 6 months on a Standard Visitor Visa.
- United States: Up to 6 months on a B-2 tourist visa.
Always verify with the destination country's embassy as durations may change.

Visa on Arrival
Passengers applying for a Visa on Arrival need these documents: valid passport, return flight ticket, proof of funds, completed arrival form, passport photograph, and visa fee payment.
Countries offering Visa on Arrival to Indian passport holders include Thailand, Sri Lanka, Maldives, and Indonesia (verify current eligibility before travel).
